Electronics

Electronic adhesive solutions: built for the future of devices

Electronics manufacturing is defined by relentless miniaturisation. Devices must become thinner and lighter while integrating more functions, faster processors and larger batteries. Every gram and every millimetre matters — and adhesive tapes have quietly become one of the enabling technologies behind this progress, replacing screws, rivets and liquid glues with thin, precise, high-performance bonds. [Placeholder copy — DEON narrative to be added.]

As a manufacturer and converter, we participate across the electronics value chain: from component-level bonding on the printed circuit board, through module and display assembly, to the protection and sealing of the finished device. Our die-cutting and converting capabilities turn base tapes into application-ready parts, supplied to the exact geometry each assembly line requires. [Placeholder copy — DEON narrative to be added.]

The result is a single supplier for mounting, bonding, insulating, shielding, masking and thermal-management tapes — backed by application engineering, qualification support and reliable global availability. [Placeholder copy — DEON narrative to be added.]

Features

Key features of our electronic adhesives

Electronic assemblies demand far more than a strong bond. A tape may need to dissipate heat, block moisture, insulate against current, survive reflow temperatures and still be removable for rework — often all within the same device. Our portfolio is engineered around these competing requirements. [Placeholder copy — DEON narrative to be added.]

  • Bonding performance
  • Impact resistance
  • Residue-free removal
  • Anti-repulsion
  • Low-surface-energy (LSE) performance
  • Long-term reliability
  • Chemical resistance
  • Conformability
  • Electrical conductivity
  • Thermal management
  • Moisture blocking
  • Outgassing resistance
